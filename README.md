## What Is This?

Meridian is a healthcare quality-management platform. It gives clinical and administrative teams a single place to monitor quality metrics, log and review incidents, and track regulatory compliance through dashboards and reports.

---

## Features

| Feature | Description | Status |
|---|---|:---:|
| Quality dashboards | KPIs and trends at a glance | ✅ |
| Incident tracking | Log, triage, and review incidents | ✅ |
| Compliance tracking | Standards and audit readiness | 🚧 |
| Reporting | Exportable quality reports | 🚧 |
| Role-based access | Permissions by team and role | 🚧 |

---
